 Here are the general ranges for some common health parameters:
1. Blood Pressure:
   - Normal: Systolic <120 mmHg and Diastolic <80 mmHg
   - High (Hypertension): Systolic ≥140 mmHg or Diastolic ≥90 mmHg

2. Blood Sugar/Glucose Level:
   - Normal Fasting Blood Sugar: 70-100 mg/dL or 3.9-5.6 mmol/L
   - High Blood Sugar (Hyperglycemia): Fasting >125 mg/dL or 7.0 mmol/L
   - Low Blood Sugar (Hypoglycemia): <70 mg/dL or <3.9 mmol/L

3. Body Temperature:
   - Normal: Around 98.6°F or 37°C (may vary slightly between individuals)

4. Heart Rate/Pulse:
   - Normal Resting Heart Rate: 60-100 bpm (beats per minute)

5. Cholesterol Levels:
   - Total Cholesterol:
     - Desirable: <200 mg/dL
     - High: ≥240 mg/dL

   - LDL (Low-Density Lipoprotein) Cholesterol:
     - Optimal: <100 mg/dL
     - High: ≥160 mg/dL

   - HDL (High-Density Lipoprotein) Cholesterol:
     - Good: >40 mg/dL for men, >50 mg/dL for women
     - Low: <40 mg/dL for men, <50 mg/dL for women

6. Body Mass Index (BMI):
   - Underweight: <18.5 kg/m²
   - Normal Weight: 18.5-24.9 kg/m²
   - Overweight: 25-29.9 kg/m²
   - Obesity: ≥30 kg/m²
